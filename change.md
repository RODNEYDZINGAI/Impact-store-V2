Because opportunity shouldn't be locked behind a job listing either.

## 1. Refund & Exchange Policy

**Page URL:** megabyteco.co.za/refund-policy

### Overview

MegaByteCo is committed to fair and transparent trading. This policy governs all return, exchange, and refund requests and is aligned with the South African Consumer Protection Act (CPA), Act 68 of 2008.

### Returns & Exchanges

Returns and exchanges are accepted under the following conditions only:

- The product is defective, damaged, or materially different from what was ordered
- The return request is submitted within 7 (seven) calendar days of the delivery date
- The item is returned in its original condition with all accessories, packaging, and proof of purchase
- Change-of-mind returns are not accepted and will not be processed

### What Is Not Covered

- Physical damage, liquid damage, or electrical damage caused after delivery
- Devices that have been tampered with, rooted, jailbroken, or repaired by an unauthorised party
- Accessories, cables, and chargers — non-returnable once opened
- Software issues, data loss, or viruses
- Products purchased under lay-buy (governed by the separate Lay-Buy Policy)

### CPA Compliance — Section 56

In accordance with Section 56 of the Consumer Protection Act, if a product is found to be defective within 6 (six) months of the date of delivery, the customer is entitled to request a repair, replacement, or refund.

MegaByteCo will assess each claim individually. Where a defect is confirmed, MegaByteCo will offer one of the following at its discretion: repair, replacement with an equivalent product, or a store credit. Cash refunds are issued only where repair and replacement are not reasonably possible.

### Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 7–14 business days via the original payment method. Delivery, handling, and administrative fees are non-refundable unless the fault is proven to lie with MegaByteCo.

### How to Submit a Return Request

Contact us via WhatsApp or email before sending any item back. Items returned without prior written authorisation will not be accepted and will be returned to sender at the customer's expense.

## 4. Warranty Policy

**Page URL:** megabyteco.co.za/warranty-policy

### Overview

This policy defines the warranty terms applicable to all products sold by MegaByteCo.

### Warranty Period

- All listed devices carry a 3-month carry-in warranty from the date of delivery or collection, unless otherwise stated on the invoice
- For standard purchases: warranty commences on the date of delivery
- For lay-buy purchases: warranty commences on the date of item release after full payment

### What Is Covered

- Hardware faults and manufacturing defects occurring under normal use
- Screen and battery defects present at the time of sale — must be reported within 48 hours of receipt

### What Is NOT Covered

- Physical damage — cracked screens, bent frames, broken ports
- Liquid or moisture damage of any kind
- Electrical damage caused by non-standard chargers or power surges
- Damage caused by misuse, dropping, or neglect
- Devices opened, repaired, or modified by any party other than MegaByteCo or an authorised repair centre
- Software issues, viruses, or data loss
- Normal wear and tear — minor scratches, fading, or cosmetic ageing
- Accessories including chargers, cables, cases, and earphones

### How to Claim

Contact MegaByteCo via WhatsApp or email within the warranty period before sending or bringing in any device. Do not attempt to repair the device yourself or take it to a third-party repair shop — doing so will void the warranty immediately and no claim will be considered.

The customer is responsible for courier costs to return the device for assessment. If the fault is confirmed to be covered under warranty, MegaByteCo will cover the return courier costs.

### Assessment & Resolution

MegaByteCo will assess the device within 5–7 business days of receipt. Where the fault is covered, MegaByteCo will repair or replace the device at its discretion. If the fault is found to be excluded from warranty coverage, the customer will be provided with a repair quotation.

### Limitation of Liability

MegaByteCo does not provide extended or lifetime warranties. Our warranty obligations are strictly limited to repair or replacement of the defective product within the stated warranty period.
